A Dream That Has Been Cooking For Generations

We believe that every restaurant has its heart and it’s the kitchen. Pomo means kitchen in Italian and in our restaurant & pizzeria it is open for every guest. The way food is prepared and made is important.

What if you could actually see how your dish is created by our chefs? You can see it! We have an open kitchen which means all the action is there and you can enjoy it when sitting on your table.

Our menu is versatile. You have fantastic meat dishes, salads and pastas. Do we have pizzas? Of course we do! Actually we have really special ones, which are called Neapolitan style pizzas. Come and try!
